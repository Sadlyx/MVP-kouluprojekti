Selecting the anti-virus program

The most important feature for antivirus software is, of course, the ability to protect your computer from viruses. Most users will also appreciate the ease of use and task automation. Small impact on the performance of the program is an important feature for people who are using older computers. Our team recommends one of the following three options.

Avast free antivirus

A good malware detection, password manager with many useful features such as the update program scanning.

AVG antivirus free

A good malware detection, fast scanning, website security rating.

Panda free antivirus

A good malware detection, easy to use interface, process monitor.
